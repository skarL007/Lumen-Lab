Our method

Study software should earn your trust.

An add-on touches a collection you may have built for years. Every decision starts with data safety and ends with evidence.

View the engine-generated theme showcase →
  1. 01

    Audit before redesigning

    We reproduce defects against Anki's real engine. Correctness comes before polish.

  2. 02

    Privacy without configuration

    No CDN, telemetry or cloud dependency. Your study data remains yours.

  3. 03

    Changes ask for permission

    Template migrations are opt-in, compared before applying and protected by backup.
